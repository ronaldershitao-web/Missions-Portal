ℹ About this Dashboard

Mission Intelligence Centre provides ministry leaders with an overview of the health of the Missions Department through registrations, attendance, church engagement and participant journeys.

The dashboard currently analyses data collected through Missions Department Events. Future versions will incorporate:

  • Short-Term Mission Trips
  • Mission Team Deployment
  • Missionary Development Pathways
  • Volunteer & Team Leader Analytics
  • Financial & Giving Analytics
  • Long-term Ministry Impact
